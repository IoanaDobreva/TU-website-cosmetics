Plants with Cosmetic Uses

Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis)

It contains anti-inflammatory chemical compounds — which reduce swelling — called anthraquinones.
These chemical compounds promote healing and alleviate pain when applied to your skin.

Cannabis (CBD)

CBD has an anti-inflammatory effect on sebocytes. Helps reduce inflammation. As an anti-inflammatory,
CBD helps swelling, pain, and redness from existing breakouts,
or irritation from skin conditions like eczema and psoriasis.

Lavender (Lavandula)

Lavender oil works to kill bacteria, and this can prevent and heal acne breakouts.
It unclogs pores and reduces inflammation when you put it on your skin.
